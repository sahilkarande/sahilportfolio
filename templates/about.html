About me

I am a Computer Engineering graduate currently enrolled in the Diploma in Big Data Analytics (DBDA) program at CDAC Mumbai-Kharghar. With a strong foundation in data science, analytics, and data engineering, I bring hands-on experience from internships at Solar Industries India Ltd. and Clustor Computing. At Solar Industries, I contributed as an IoT Intern by improving manufacturing processes through real-time dashboards using Power BI and Excel. At Clustor Computing, I focused on creating dynamic dashboards and visual reports to support business intelligence.

My technical journey includes key academic and personal projects such as “InsightHub: Your Personal Data Analytics Companion,” a Streamlit-based data analysis platform, and “Stock Market Prediction of BSE and NSE Companies” using ML algorithms. I am currently working on end-to-end case studies involving Linear and Logistic Regression, Decision Trees, and Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) using Python, pandas, seaborn, and scikit-learn. My expertise continues to grow in big data tools such as Hadoop, Spark, Kafka, and MongoDB, integrated with platforms like AWS and S3 for scalable solutions.

As the Technical Class Representative at CDAC, I actively contribute to academic coordination and project mentoring. My leadership experience is complemented by previous involvement with Google Developer Student Clubs (GDSC), where I organized impactful seminars and workshops. I remain committed to continuous learning and development in data engineering, cloud computing, and machine learning technologies.

Thank you for visiting my portfolio. I am eager to connect with like-minded professionals and collaborate on innovative solutions in the evolving landscape of big data and AI-driven analytics.
